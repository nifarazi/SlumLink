Field Agent Support System

On-ground assistance for every community

Field Agents are trained representatives of SlumLink who work directly inside slum communities. They help residents report issues, access aid, and communicate effectively with NGOs and local authorities.

By combining digital tracking with physical presence, Field Agents ensure that no household is left unheard or unsupported.
