About SlumLink

A single platform for verified records, smarter outreach, and measurable progress—built for households, NGOs, and local government teams.

Make families visible

Households register once and get a QR-linked identity that keeps support fair and trackable.

Make aid accountable

NGOs create campaigns with eligibility rules and record distribution with QR scans.

Make issues actionable

Residents raise complaints and local government can move them from submitted to resolved.

For Households

Create a family profile, get a QR identity, and track support and complaints.

What happens after registration

  • Submit household details (members, income range, education, skills).
  • Receive a unique QR-linked ID.
  • Get notified when you’re eligible for nearby aid events.
  • Scan QR at events → aid is recorded in your history.
  • Submit complaints and track status updates.

For Non-Profit Organization

Plan campaigns, scan QR at events, and measure how many people you helped.

How it works for NGOs

  • Create a campaign (food, medical, education, training).
  • Set eligibility (age range, area/slum, conditions).
  • On event day, scan QR codes to record distributions.
  • View clear summaries: people helped, areas covered, monthly breakdowns.
